Genome Explorer

I work at VIDI Labs with Chris Bryan and Dr. Kwan Liu Ma on a genome visualization that I started a year ago and maintain to this day. The Java/Processing application we made allows users, to see the DNA that extant mammals, like the Dog and Chimpanzee, have in common on a chromosomal level. The visualization is designed for both genomics domain experts and lay-users alike. It makes use of Contiguous Ancestral Region (CAR) data, which recently became available thanks to a breakthrough in Bioinformatics. As more CAR data becomes available, our visualization research will become more useful.
